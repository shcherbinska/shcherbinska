SHCHERBINSKAYA
OLGA

Was born in 1988 (Minsk,Belarus)

2003-2006 - Ahremchik Art College, Minsk, Belarus(speciality - painting)
2006-2012 - Belarusian State Academy of Arts, Minsk, Belarus(speciality - set and costume design )
Since 2012- a scenographer in Grodno drama theatre (Grodno, Belarus)
Since 2013 - a member of the youth section of The Belarusian Union of Artists
