Biography. Realized drama and puppet productions.

Olga Shcherbinskaya

artist/set and costume designer

(born 1988, Minsk, Belarus)

2003–2006 — Ahremchik College of Arts (specialty – painting), Minsk

2006–2012 — Belarusian State Academy of Arts (specialty – set and costume design), Minsk

2012–2020 — a scenographer in the Grodno Drama Theater

Since 2021 — a teacher and an author of the course in the children's art studio (Grodno)

Since 2013 — a member of the Belarusian Union of Artists
